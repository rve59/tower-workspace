
## OBJECTIVE:
Illustrate feasibility of the technology stack for the TOWER-C version of the application concept.
## PURPOSE:

The main purpose of this card is to provide a simple web application on the basis of an extensible design that can for now illustrate the ingestion and validation of a suite of records (Forms or ERQ) that are based on a transpiled XULE formula.
## SOLUTION:

I have decided to go with a Layout Specification Language that will allow scaffolding and prototyping of the UI/UX to a fairly solid degree.
